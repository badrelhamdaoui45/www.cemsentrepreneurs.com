Startup Challenge 2023 🚀

Join the 7th edition of the challenge for a chance to get expert coaching, to participate at panel discussions and to pitch your idea in front of investors and seasoned founders at the Demo Day on November 11th (16-18h), at the CEMS Career Forum in Barcelona (ESADE).

Registratios for startups are CLOSED!
Registrations for attendees are NOW OPEN!

AUDIENCE TICKET
Pitch your idea to investors
Pitch in front of investors, corporate partners, seasoned partners and CEMSies!
Win valuable prizes
Prizes for the winning teams and 0.5 ECTS for all participating students
Get expert coaching
Receive mentoring from experienced entrepreneurs

This challenge is for you if you are a CEMS student or fresh graduate (<2yrs) who is working on a business idea/startup that is aiming to provide a solution in a market with potential.

Key Dates

Application Opening
20th July

Application Deadline
30th September

Final Selection
10th October

Pitch Event
‍11th November

Requirements

(1) Being a CEMS student or fresh graduate (<2 yrs);
(2) Working on a startup idea or Being the founder of a startup;
(3) Coming to the CEMS Career Forum

If you feel you are at a later stage, refer to the investor pitch event !!

Main deliverables

(1) One page executive summary of your startup idea
(2) Investor pitch deck presentation with a maximum of 20 slides
(3) short pitch video (optional)

Evaluation

If you are building your pitch deck make sure to keep in mind the following aspects, as this is what the jury will be looking for: 
‍

Team
‍Personal and professional diversity, clear responsibilities, passion, motivation, know-how

Product / service
‍
Feasibility, innovativeness, problem-solution fit, sustainable value creation

Market
‍Market size and dynamics, positioning, USP, sustainable competitive advantage

Business model
‍Value proposition, customers, resources, cost & revenue structure

Traction
‍Achievements, pilot customers, partners
